Career helper

Why?

More often than not, people doesn't have clear guideline about how to progress with their career. Intention of this tool is to give you a guideline on how do you progress with your career and to track your progress. Remember, in software engineering, it's not just about code, to progress, you will have to be good at various fields.

You will be asked a lot of questions, and you will be presented with your result.
Since this is not an exact science, we can't guarantee you with 100% accuracy your level, but we do our best to give you the guideline. Ask Ducky bot for help :)
